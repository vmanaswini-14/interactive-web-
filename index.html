About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social change through education, community development, environmental sustainability, women empowerment, and welfare initiatives. Our mission is to inspire individuals and communities to work together toward building a better future.

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual has equal opportunities, support, and a better quality of life.

Our Mission

To actively work on ground-level initiatives such as education programs, skill development, environmental awareness, and community support to bring meaningful change in people’s lives.

Our Values

Compassion, teamwork, integrity, inclusiveness, and commitment toward positive change.

Our Projects

Project SEVA

Providing food and clothing support to underprivileged communities and spreading compassion through service.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Helping children gain access to quality education, digital literacy, and opportunities for a brighter future.

Project JEEV

Supporting animal welfare through rescue efforts, protection and feeding initiatives.

Project UDDAN

Empowering women through skill development, awareness programs and financial independence.

Project PRAKRITI

Encouraging environmental sustainability through tree plantation and green initiatives.

Project VIKAS

Providing internships and skill development programs to help students build careers and leadership skills.
